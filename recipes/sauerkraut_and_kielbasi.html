Sauerkraut and Kielbasi

Ingredients

  • 1 jars Polish sauerkraut
  • 42oz. Polish kielbasi (sliced thinly into rounds)
  • 2 slices of bacon
  • 1 large yellow onion (diced)
  • 5 cloves of garlic (diced)
  • 1 tsp kosher salt
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tbsp paprika
  • 3-4 bay leaves

Procedure

Thoroughly rinse sauerkraut to remove bitterness and set aside.

Cook bacon for the grease. Remove bacon and saute diced onions. Once onions are translucent add garlic continue cooking. Add sauerkraut, two jars of water, and stir in spices. Simmer on low for 6-8 hours or transfer to a slow cooker.

Once the sauerkraut melts in your mouth add the kielbasi and cook for an hour. Skim any excess grease before serving.
